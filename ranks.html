Ranking Method

Total Score Calculation:

Total Score = Trophy Points + Medal Points + Individual Award Points + Win Points

Trophy Points:

Each trophy has specific points based on its importance:

  • FIFA World Cup: 100 points
  • UEFA Champions League: 90 points
  • FIFA Club World Cup: 80 points
  • UEFA Europa League: 70 points
  • Premier League: 60 points
  • UEFA Nations League: 55 points
  • UEFA Conference League: 50 points
  • FA Cup: 45 points
  • UEFA Super Cup: 40 points
  • Community Shield: 30 points

Medal Points:

Medals are awarded for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place finishes:

  • 🥇 Gold Medal = Trophy Points ÷ 2
  • 🥈 Silver Medal = Gold Medal Points ÷ 2
  • 🥉 Bronze Medal = Silver Medal Points ÷ 2

Individual Award Points:

  • Golden Boot (Top Scorer): Trophy Points ÷ 4
    • Example: UCL Golden Boot = 90 ÷ 4 = 22.5 points
  • Golden Glove (Best Goalkeeper): Trophy Points ÷ 2
    • Example: Premier League Golden Glove = 60 ÷ 2 = 30 points
  • Ballon d'Or: 80 points (fixed)
  • Yashin Trophy (Best Goalkeeper): 80 points (fixed)

Win Points:

Each match win = 1 point (including forfeit wins)

Forfeit Rules:

  • Forfeit Win: Counts as 1 win, but NO goals are credited (GS and GC stay 0)
  • Forfeit Loss: Counts as 1 loss, concede 3 goals, but GS stays 0
  • No Contest: Counts only toward Matches Played, no W/D/L or goals

Tiebreaker Order:

  1. Highest Total Score
  2. Most Trophies
  3. Most Wins
  4. Best Goal Difference

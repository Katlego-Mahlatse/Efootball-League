ARTICLE 1. LEAGUE STRUCTURE
1.1 The league shall consist of ten (14) teams.
1.2 The competition shall be conducted in a double round-robin format.
1.3 Each team shall play every other team twice — once home and once away — resulting in twenty six (26) matches per team.
1.4 The official fixture list shall be provided to all participants and shall serve as the binding schedule.
ARTICLE 2. MATCH SCHEDULING AND COMPLETION
2.1 Participants are responsible for arranging their matches through the official group chat.
2.2 Every match must be completed and the final result posted in the group chat no later than 23:00 (11:00 PM) on the scheduled matchday.
2.3 No-Show and Forfeit Procedure
a) If one participant actively attempts to communicate and arrange the match but the opponent fails to respond or appear by the 23:00 deadline, the match shall be awarded as a 3-0 victory to the communicating participant. The winning participant shall receive 3 points, and the defaulting participant shall receive 0 points and a 0-3 defeat.
b) If neither participant initiates communication or makes any effort to schedule or play the match by 23:00 on the scheduled day, the match shall be recorded as a 0-0 result with no points awarded to either team. Both teams receive 0 points.
ARTICLE 3. POINTS SYSTEM
3.1 A win shall award three (3) points to the victorious team.
3.2 A draw shall award one (1) point to each team.
3.3 A loss shall award zero (0) points.
ARTICLE 4. TIEBREAKER RULES
In the event two or more teams finish with the same number of points, the following tiebreakers shall be applied in this order:
4.1 Goal Difference (GD)
4.2 Total Goals Scored (GF)
4.3 Head-to-head points earned in matches between the tied teams
4.4 Head-to-head Goal Difference
4.5 Final decision by the CEOs.
ARTICLE 5. TEAM SELECTION AND ELIGIBILITY
5.1 Participants may select any official real-world club team for the duration of the season.
5.2 The use of Dream Teams, fantasy squads, Icon teams, or any squads containing custom or created players is strictly prohibited.
5.3 Only authentic, official real-world club squads are permitted.
5.4 Once a team is selected, it cannot be changed or swapped for the remainder of the season. Doing so forcefully may result in Point Deductions
ARTICLE 6. RESPECT & FAIR PLAY
6.1 Use appropriate language at all times.
6.2 Match settings should be made fair for both teams.
If you object with any of the rules kindly state your objection in the group and it will be addressed. Visca Barça
